Here are some special characters used in HTML:
&
<
>
©
®
™

Explanation

Comments in HTML are written within <!-- --> tags and are not displayed on the page. Special characters are represented using HTML entities, for example:

  • &amp; for &
  • &lt; for <
  • &gt; for >
  • &copy; for ©
  • &reg; for ®
  • &trade; for ™
